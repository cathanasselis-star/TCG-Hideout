About the TCG

What is Pokémon TCG?

The Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) is a competitive strategy game where two players face off as Pokémon Trainers, using custom-built 60-card decks filled with Pokémon, Trainer, and Energy cards. The primary goal is to Knock Out your opponent’s Pokémon to take Prize cards; the first player to collect all six Prize cards wins the match. With a rich history dating back to 1996, the game constantly evolves with new expansions and mechanics—such as the recent Tera Pokémon ex—making it a favorite for both hardcore competitive players and casual collectors worldwide.



Basic Game Mechanics

  • Deck: Every player needs a 60-card deck.
  • Prize Cards: Take all 6 prize cards to win.
  • Energy: Attached to Pokémon to power attacks.
  • Evolution: Evolve Basic Pokémon into Stage 1 and Stage 2.
